The Alejandro's Movies Ranking

My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

GLADIATOR

Gladiator (Gladiador en Hispanoamérica y Gladiator en España) es una película épica del género péplum y acción del año 2000 dirigida por Ridley Scott y protagonizada por Russell Crowe, Joaquin Phoenix y Connie Nielsen. Crowe interpreta a Máximo Décimo Meridio, un leal general hispano del ejército del Imperio romano que es traicionado por Cómodo, el ambicioso hijo del emperador Marco Aurelio, quien ha asesinado a su padre y se ha hecho con el trono. Forzado a convertirse en esclavo, Máximo triunfa como gladiador mientras anhela vengar la muerte de su familia y la del emperador.

HACKSAW RIDGE

"Hacksaw Ridge" is a 2016 biographical war drama film directed by Mel Gibson. The film tells the true story of Desmond Doss, a World War II Army medic who served during the Battle of Okinawa. Doss, who was a conscientious objector due to his religious beliefs, refused to carry a weapon but still became a hero by saving the lives of 75 men during the battle.

A BEAUTIFUL MIND

"A Beautiful Mind" is a biographical drama film released in 2001, directed by Ron Howard. The movie is loosely based on the life of John Nash, a brilliant mathematician and Nobel laureate in Economics. The film stars Russell Crowe as John Nash and portrays his struggles with schizophrenia and his contributions to game theory.
